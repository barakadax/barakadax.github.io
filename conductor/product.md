# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
Portfolio to show a bit about myself & a lot of what I can do.

## Target Audience
- Recruiters and Hiring Managers

## Goals
- Showcase my technical skills and expertise
- Display my projects and open-source contributions

## Key Features
- A project showcase with filtering (by language, technology)
- An "About Me" section with skills and experience
- A blog or articles section
